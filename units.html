Units

The Enthusiast

The Enthusiast townhouse is perfect for those who own up to 4 cars. Enjoy a comfortable and spacious living area while keeping your prized cars close and secure.

The Gear Head

For the more dedicated car lovers, the Gear Head townhouse offers ample space for up to 6 cars. Experience the joy of living surrounded by your beloved vehicles.

The Collector

If you have a growing collection of cars, the Collector townhouse can store up to 10 cars with ease. This type is ideal for serious collectors who value both style and functionality.

Bespoke

For those with even larger collections or specific requirements, our Bespoke units can be customized to your exact specifications. These tailored townhouses can accommodate more than 10 cars and are designed to meet your unique needs.
