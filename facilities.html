Facilities

Gear up for an adrenaline-pumping stay at Boosted Hostels! We aren't just a place to crash after a long journey; we're a full-throttle haven for car enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Our state-of-the-art facilities offer something for every type of traveler:

  1. Boosted Raceway

    Get your racing fix on our very own private track! Perfect for honing your skills, testing your limits, or simply enjoying a thrilling ride. The best thing about BOOSTED's track is the Paddock! Whether you're a seasoned mechanic or a curious newcomer, our automotive shop is your pit stop for tinkering and TLC. Equipped with top-notch tools and knowledgeable staff, it's the perfect place to fix up your ride or learn a thing or two.

    2. Throttle Country Club

    When it's time to relax and unwind, the clubhouse is your oasis. Take a refreshing dip in the pool, shoot some hoops on the basketball court, or challenge your travel buddies to a tennis match. Throttle Country Club is also the home to our very own gym, filled with the best equipment you could use to train and stay fit.

    The Park

    Need a moment to unwind and soak up the scenery? Our serene park offers a tranquil escape. Stretch out on the grass, have a picnic under the open sky, or simply connect with nature and fellow travelers.
